## 3. App Store Metadata (Marketing)

### **Promotional Text**
> Pro-grade MLB analysis in your pocket. Visual percentile rankings, advanced leaderboards, and nightly tracking for every player in the league.

### **Description**
> **Scout the league like a pro.**
> 
> StatScout translates complex MLB pitch-tracking data into intuitive, visual percentile rankings. Built for the modern fan who wants to see beyond the basic box score, StatScout provides a mobile-first look at the metrics that truly drive the game.
> 
> **VISUAL PERCENTILES**
> Instantly see where players rank among their peers in xwOBA, Hard-Hit%, Sprint Speed, and OAA using our signature red-and-blue Savant-style visual bars.
> 
> **LIVE LEADERBOARDS**
> Filter the entire league across Hitting, Pitching, Running, and Fielding. Find out who is leading the league in the specific metrics that matter most.
> 
> **PLAYER PROFILES**
> Comprehensive data for every active player. Switch between advanced percentile metrics and standard season stats with a single tap.
> 
> **NIGHTLY UPDATES**
> Our data feed refreshes every night, ensuring you have the latest rankings and game logs as the season unfolds.
> 
> **PRIVACY-FIRST**
> No accounts. No tracking. No ads. Just the data you need.
> 
> StatScout is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by Major League Baseball or any individual team.

## 4. App Privacy (Data Safety)
When completing the **App Privacy** section in Apple Connect, use these selections to match the *Vitals* style:

*   **Data Collection:** Select **"No, we do not collect data from this app."**
