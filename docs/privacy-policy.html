Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Overview

StatScout is a mobile-first MLB analytics app that provides visual percentile rankings and leaderboards. Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built.

Data Collection

StatScout does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data.

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone) are collected
  • No search history or location data is tracked
  • No analytics or tracking pixels are used
  • No advertising or third-party SDKs
  • No account creation required

Data Usage

StatScout fetches publicly available baseball statistics from our remote database. This request transmits standard technical information (such as your IP address) necessary for the server to fulfill the request. This technical data is not linked to any personal identity and is not used for tracking.

Local Storage

Your app preferences (such as selected categories) are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.

Children's Privacy

StatScout does not knowingly collect information from children. The app does not collect information from any user.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact:
