Frequently Asked Questions

How often is data updated?

StatScout data feeds refresh nightly after all MLB games have concluded. This ensures you have the most up-to-date percentile rankings and season stats every morning.

Where does the data come from?

Our percentile rankings are calculated using public pitch-tracking data. We aggregate this data to provide a visual, easy-to-read comparison of every player in the league.

Is my data private?

Yes. StatScout does not collect any personal information, search history, or location data. Your app preferences are stored locally on your device. Read our Privacy Policy →

What devices are supported?

StatScout is built for iPhone and requires iOS 17 or later.

Do I need an account?

No. StatScout works right out of the box with no sign-up, login, or subscription required.
